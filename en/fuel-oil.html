Professional approach to
hazardous waste

Contaminated fuel oil or fuel oil with impurities is a low-quality fuel that contains excessive moisture, mechanical contaminants, or foreign components, which significantly reduces its energy value and complicates its use.

Contaminated fuel oil is fuel oil in which: the water content exceeds the permissible limit (more than 1–2%), and an emulsion of "water-oil" may be present, which is difficult to separate.

Fuel oil with impurities contains:
mechanical contaminants (sand, rust, sludge), residues of acids, alkalis, oils or other petroleum products, degradation products (due to improper storage or overheating).

Why this is a problem:
Reduced calorific value — fuel oil loses up to 30% of its heat when there is excess water; Clogging of fuel systems — impurities accumulate on nozzles and filters.

Increased wear of equipment — corrosion, soot, deterioration of boiler and engine performance.
Environmental threat — spillage or leakage contaminates soil and water.

If your organisation is faced with the need to dispose of fuel oil — contact us. We know how to do it properly.

How does it work?

Purification from impurities and degassing.

Collection and safe storage of fuel oil.

Processing into fuel or industrial products in accordance with environmental safety standards.

Why is this important?

Proper disposal of fuel oil reduces the risk of air, soil, and water pollution, protecting human health and the environment.

At the same time, processing allows for the rational use of hydrocarbons, reducing dependence on primary energy sources.

Fuel oil is the "black gold" waiting for a second life.

Despite its dark appearance, fuel oil contains up to 70% of the energy of crude oil. Thanks to modern technologies, its processing allows us to restore this potential and use it in industry instead of fossil fuels.

The disposal of fuel oil is a fight against a "silent ecological disaster."

Uncontrolled storage of fuel oil leads to soil and water pollution, as well as emissions of harmful volatile compounds. Every ton of disposed fuel oil is a real contribution to nature conservation and human health.

Processing fuel oil is not only about ecology but also a strategic advantage for business.

Timely disposal and processing help companies save hundreds of thousands of hryvnias in fines, avoid production stoppages, and enhance competitiveness through "green" technologies.
