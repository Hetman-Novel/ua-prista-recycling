Full cycle of waste oil (oil)
management:

Laboratory analysis

We carry out detailed laboratory analysis of waste oil products, which allows us to accurately determine their physical and chemical characteristics, the presence of toxic or harmful impurities, and to establish the hazard class in accordance with national and international standards

Based on the data obtained, we can reasonably choose the most appropriate approach to further treatment or disposal of such waste.

Recycling and disposal

Depending on the composition and hazardousness of the waste, we use efficient and environmentally friendly treatment technologies. This may include recovery for further use, thermal disposal in specialised emission-controlled facilities, chemical or biological treatment.

Our goal is to minimise the negative impact on the environment, minimise residual substances and ensure full safe disposal.

Procurement and logistics

We organise a complete logistics cycle: from the departure to the customer and the collection of waste to its delivery to enterprises for recycling. The entire process complies with safety standards and environmental requirements. Proper packaging, labelling, and the preparation of transport documentation are ensured.

We coordinate transport routes and monitor compliance with all requirements during transportation.

Legal support

For each client, we provide comprehensive legal support — preparing all necessary documentation, including contracts, acceptance-transfer acts, declarations, and reports.

We ensure that all operations comply with the current legislation of Ukraine in the field of waste management.

International compliance

We ensure full compliance of all procedures with international standards, including the Basel Convention. We prepare a full package of documents for cross-border transport, customs clearance and interaction with foreign partners, taking into account all environmental safety requirements.

This guarantees the legality and transparency of all operations, even when operating outside the country.

What we deal with: a list of
wastes we dispose of and
recycle

Bitumen

A residue of petroleum distillation that is used in construction but becomes hazardous if improperly stored or released.

Used oils

Used engine, transmission and other technical oils containing heavy metals and toxic impurities.

Cubic residue

Concentrated residues after distillation of petroleum products containing toxic compounds.

Sour tar and tar

Thick residues from oil refining with a high content of sulphur and acids that are hazardous to the environment.

Fuel oil

A heavy petroleum product that can pose a threat to soil and water if it leaks.

Oil products

Fuels, oils, lubricants, solvents, etc. that have lost their consumer properties or are mixed with waste.

Oil waste

A mixture of materials containing petroleum products that arise in production and service.

Oil sludge

Thick sediment that accumulates at the bottom of tanks, ponds or sludge ponds, polluting the environment.

Technical residues

Sediments and contaminants that remain on the walls of tanks after storage of petroleum products.
